# Explanation Contract

## Purpose

The Explanation Contract defines the **minimum standard of explanation**
required for any recommendation produced by APE.

It exists to ensure:
- clarity,
- trust,
- auditability,
- and resistance to narrative bias.

If the contract is not satisfied, the recommendation must not be presented.

---

## Mandatory Explanation Components

Every recommendation must include:

### 1. Decision Summary
A plain-language description of:
- what is being recommended,
- or why no action is recommended.

---

### 2. Relevant Portfolio State
A concise statement of:
- current allocation,
- target allocation,
- observed drift or lack thereof.

Facts only. No interpretation.

---

### 3. Policy Basis
A direct reference to:
- the relevant policy rules,
- targets,
- bands,
- or constraints that justify the decision.

This should answer:
> “Which part of the policy allowed or blocked this action?”

---

### 4. Reasoning and Trade-offs
An explanation of:
- why this option was chosen,
- what alternatives were considered,
- what was intentionally *not* done.

Trade-offs must be explicit.

---

### 5. Uncertainty and Confidence
A clear statement of:
- what is unknown or assumed,
- confidence level in the recommendation,
- conditions under which the recommendation may change.

Overconfidence is a failure mode.

---

### 6. Next Review or Trigger
A statement of:
- when this decision should be revisited,
- or what event would justify re-evaluation.

---

## Language and Tone Rules

Explanations must:
- avoid certainty where none exists,
- avoid persuasive or emotive language,
- avoid market predictions or narratives,
- prefer “because” over “despite”.

---

## Prohibited Explanation Patterns

The following invalidate an explanation:
- appeals to authority (“the model thinks…”),
- vague justifications (“markets are uncertain”),
- hindsight framing,
- omission of uncertainty.

---

## Relationship to Recommendations

The explanation is **not optional** and **not secondary**.

A recommendation without a compliant explanation
must be treated as **invalid output**.
