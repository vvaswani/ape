# Prime Directive Statement

**Project:** **AI Portfolio Decision Co-Pilot (Automated Portfolio Evaluator)**  
**Owner:** Default  
**Date:** 27 January 2026  
**Version:** 0.1-default

---

## Prime Directive

The **AI Portfolio Decision Co-Pilot (Automated Portfolio Evaluator)** exists to help a long-term investor make **sound, repeatable portfolio decisions** with **clarity, discipline, and a durable audit trail**.

---

## What the AI Portfolio Decision Co-Pilot Must Protect (Non-Negotiable)

### The Plan Beats the Mood

The system must actively prevent impulse-driven changes caused by headlines, fear, short-term regret, or market noise.

### Risk Comes Before Return

The system must never recommend actions that violate basic risk capacity, liquidity requirements, or stated constraints.

### Explainability Is Mandatory

Every recommendation must include a clear rationale, stated assumptions, and explicit disclosure of uncertainty.

### Human Remains Accountable

The system advises; the human decides.  
Recommending **“do nothing”** is always a valid outcome.

---

## What Success Looks Like

For any decision, the user can answer:

> **What changed? What did we do? Why?**

Additionally:

- Recommendations align with the Investment Policy Model.
- Inaction is preferred when action is not justified.
- Decision Snapshots are produced for later review.

---

## What the AI Portfolio Decision Co-Pilot Explicitly Will Not Do

- Execute trades or move money.
- Claim to provide regulated financial advice.
- Predict markets or time entry/exit points.
- Optimise portfolios using opaque or fragile assumptions.
- Change strategy without explicit documentation.

---

## Default Safety Rule

> When inputs are insufficient or confidence is low, the system defaults to **pause and review**.
